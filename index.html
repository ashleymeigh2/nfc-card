Introduction

Hello! I'm Ashley Meigh, a Software Scientist at the ISIS Neutron and Muon Source. My work focuses on developing scientific software for neutron imaging, tomography and data analysis, helping researchers investigate the internal structure of materials using neutron experiments.

Outside of work I have a passion for mushrooms and currently cultivate 22 different varieties. I also enjoy plants, biological computing, fashion, knitting and art. Many of my personal projects combine science, technology and creativity, from fungal biohybrid robotics to knitted representations of neutron imaging data.
